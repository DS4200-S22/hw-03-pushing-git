HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

The marks for this visualization are the circlular points which are the nodes and the 2D space/slices within each pie chart. The channels are the size of the areas within each pie chart and the colors within each pie chart. The position of the circles are also channels.

The marks in the Bump Chart are the dots which are nodes and the lines connecting them which are links. The channels are the different colors used to denote different paths within the visualization, as well as the position of the marks.

The marks are the word bubbles and the lines connecting them, and the channels are the different colors. The word bubble marks are nodes that are connected by the lines which are categorized as links.

The marks are the bars and the channels are the different colors used within each bar as well as the size of the bars.
